Technology Articles


Artificial Intelligence (AI)
The simulation of human intelligence by machines.
It allows systems to think, learn, and adapt through data and experience.
AI is used in self-driving cars, healthcare, voice assistants, and smart devices.
   

Web Development
Web development focuses on building and maintaining websites and web apps.
It combines front-end tools like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript with back-end technologies like Python, PHP, and databases.
   

Cybersecurity
Cybersecurity protects computers, networks, and data from theft, damage, or unauthorized access.
It includes using firewalls, encryption, and secure passwords to defend systems.
